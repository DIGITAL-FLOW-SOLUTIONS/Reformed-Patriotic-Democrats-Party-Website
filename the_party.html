REPA Party Manifesto

The Reformed Patriotic Democrats (REPA) is dedicated to creating a fair, inclusive, and thriving Kenya by making sure everyone has equal access to opportunities and resources. Our manifesto presents key policies and strategies to lead Kenya towards economic growth, effective governance, and social justice.

REPA Party Ideology

What We Believe In

The Reformed Patriotic Democrats (REPA) believe in equality for all, meaning everyone should have the same rights and opportunities. We focus on social justice, fairness, and inclusivity, ensuring that our leadership benefits every Kenyan, no matter their background, gender, ethnicity, or economic status. Our goal is to build a transparent, accountable government that serves all citizens, not just a select few.
