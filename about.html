History of the party

Our Story

The Reformed Patriotic Democrats (REPA) is a political party started in February 2023. Our goal is to build a fair society where every Kenyan has equal rights, dignity, and opportunities. Formed by activists, scholars, and community leaders, REPA aims to represent all Kenyans, not just a select few. We believe fairness, justice, and inclusive governance are essential rights, and we are committed to making these values a reality throughout Kenya.

WHY US

We stand for inclusive leadership, social justice, and fair governance, and we are committed to transforming Kenya into a society where democracy, equality, and prosperity are shared by all.

OUR CORE VALUES

Inclusion

Every citizen deserves representation and a voice in governance.

Equity & Equality

Fair distribution of resources and opportunities.

Transparency & Accountability

Fighting corruption and promoting ethical leadership.

Social Justice & Human Rights

Upholding civil liberties and dignity for all.

Rule of Law

Strengthening institutions to serve all Kenyans fairly.

Integrity

Committing to truth, honesty, and responsible governance.
