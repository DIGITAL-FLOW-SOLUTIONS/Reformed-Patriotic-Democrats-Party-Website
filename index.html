Reformed Patriotic Democrats (REPA)

Welcome to the Reformed Patriotic Democrats (REPA) website. We are committed to creating a fair society in Kenya. Our goal is for everyone to have equal chances, justice, and economic strength. We value honesty, openness, and inclusion, making sure people's voices guide our country's progress. Join us in making Kenya a more just and equal place.

    Our Leadership

    Meet Our Party Leader

    Ali Omar Mwatondomera is a bold and visionary leader devoted to inclusive governance, social equity, and economic justice. As the founding leader of the Reformed Patriotic Democrats (REPA), he is passionately committed to creating a Kenya where opportunity, fairness, and dignity are guaranteed for all.

    Together, we can:

    • Build a government that works for the people.
    • Ensure every citizen enjoys equal rights and opportunities.
    • Restore dignity, unity, and accountability in leadership.

    We invite all Kenyans to be part of a bold new journey toward an inclusive, just, and empowered Kenya.

    OUR MAJOR COMMITMENTS:

    Mission

    "To work in collaboration with local and international institutions to safeguard democracy, promote social justice, and secure a better quality of life for all Kenyans."

    Vision

    "An egalitarian society where democracy and freedom prevail, with dignity and equal opportunity for all."

    Core Values

    Inclusion, Equity & Equality, Transparency & Accountability, Social Justice & Human Rights, Rule of Law and Integrity.

    Shorelines & Solidarity: Lamu County Beach Cleanup

    REPA
    /
    Solidarity

    In February 2025, party volunteers descended on Lamu’s pristine coastline for a high-impact beach cleanup in partnership with local environmental groups. Armed with gloves and garbage sacks, members combed several kilometers of beach, removing plastic waste, discarded nets, and other debris that threaten marine life and local livelihoods. The event not only restored beauty to a beloved shoreline but also showcased our commitment to environmental stewardship and community service.
